How has 3D Printing market grown due to its rise is popularity?

What is 3D Printing?

3D printing is the physical production of a digital part or object through the use of hardware and software Technologies. These final objects can be used in a variaty of applications such as prototyping, small scale manufacturing and for hobbist. 3D printing has grow in recent times due to increased coverage in the media and 3D Printers becoming significantly more affordable than 5 years ago.

Social Impact

Over 75% of people believe 3D printing to be a viable manufacturing techique next to methods such as cnc machining, injection moulding and vacuum forming. Moreover, 60% of people rated 3d printed parts greater than a 5 out of ten for quality and usability.
