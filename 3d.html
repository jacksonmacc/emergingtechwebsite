How has 3D Printing market grown due to its rise is popularity?

What is 3D Printing?

3D printing is the physical production of a digital part or object through the use of hardware and software Technologies. These final objects can be used in a variaty of applications such as prototyping, small scale manufacturing and for hobbist. 3D printing has grow in recent times due to increased coverage in the media and 3D Printers becoming significantly more affordable than 5 years ago.

Social Impact

Acording to survey and interview data, Over 75% of people believe 3D printing to be a viable manufacturing techique next to methods such as cnc machining, injection moulding and vacuum forming. Moreover, 60% of people rated 3d printed parts greater than a 5 out of ten for quality and usability.

Practical Uses

Throughout the Pandemic 3D Printing has been used to make emergency medical supplies such as parts for ventilators and personal protective equipment with some companies printing up to 500,000 items per week to help those in need.

Legal Issues

While there is nothing illegal about 3d printing, people have gotten in trouble with the law by printing items such as firearms and other unregistered weapons.

Summary

Overall the 3d Printing market has shown steady growth over recet years and has not been majorly been affected by the Covid 19 outbreak.
