Dogecoin

Dogecoin has been in the news frequently over the past few years, and it had quickly gained popularity and, more importantly, value. In fact, on the 20/5/2021, Dogecoin hit a price of $0.47453 (Yahoo Finance), from its original price of almost $0 when it launched in late 2013 (Wikipedia). It's backed by thousands, from the average person to Elon Musk himself. You will frequently see people praise it and advocate buying into it. But here lies the biggest challenge in the adoption of Dogecoin as a legitimate currency: trying to tell if people are being sarcastic or not. (just kidding)
In this section I will try to use as little sarcasm as possible and deliver you the answer to this question: How viable is Dogecoin in today's world? I will review it as a currency, a technology and, of course, as a cultural phenomenon/meme, whichever you prefer.

3D Printing

3D printing is the physical production of a digital part or object through the use of hardware and software Technologies. These final objects can be used in a variaty of applications such as prototyping, small scale manufacturing and for hobbist. 3D printing has grow in recent times due to increased coverage in the media and 3D Printers becoming significantly more affordable than 5 years ago.

Dictation

"Audio transcription itself, digital or other, is the art of turning the spoken word into the written word. Sometimes it is called digital dictation or audio typing, but these terms generally refer to ‘office correspondence’."
In this area, we will discuss the efficiency of audio transcription, or otherwise known as dictation. We will look at the three major aspects when judging something as efficient:
-Its time usage
-Its costly-ness
-The effort needed by the user for it to work
